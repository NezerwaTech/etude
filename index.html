THANKFUL HEART

God, You are good, and Your steadfast love endures forever! Even in the hardest seasons, I always have a reason to worship You. Thank You for giving me victory and abundant life in Jesus Christ! Although I don’t deserve it, You shower me with unconditional love and forgiveness. And so no matter what the future may hold, I will shout for joy because You are with me. I want my life to bring You praise.
In Jesus’ name, Amen.
